About Me

CONTACT

Hi there! I’m an ambitious Communications Design student at City Tech and a highly motivated in-house multimedia Creative Designer with 2+ years of experience in the Hospitality industry delivering creative strategy and operational solutions for Fortune 100 clients. I utilize design theory and principles to curate visual experiences with an emphasis on story telling. I’ve always been drawn to art and design ever since I was a child learning from YouTube tutorials on how to draw but decided to pursue a STEM career due to stability and societal pressure. The passion was never there and so I switched to COMD where I think there’s a good blend of tech and art. I’m inspired by modern and old architecture, renaissance paintings, and photography. My hobbies in my spare time include reading, crocheting, and archery.
